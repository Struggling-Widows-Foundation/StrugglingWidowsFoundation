WIDOWS NEED YOUR HELP

The SWF is a non profit organization, non denomination organization which strive to enable that widows have easy access to health-care expertise that are beyond their reach, empowering them, advice, support and bring them closer to their heavenly creator (GOD). Do all widows struggle? Not all struggle. Bafut is a small village in the mezam division of Bamenda Cameroon. Here most of the widows above 55years live in abject poverty, some have been abandoned by grand children to look for greener pastures in cities, some are really in critical health situation, and cannot even afford common drugs.

VISION

Our vision is to create a strong network of widows, ensuring that they should not go through
the hard times alone, but with lifelong connection and lasting support.
The foundation also aim to elucidate social health issues, pertinent to this demographic, so as to positively influence policy
and ensure that the gap between social support and social need is efficiently addressed.

MISSION

We connect people around the world in the fight to end poverty.
Working together, we invest in the lives of Struggling Widows, build the healthy environments they need to thrive, and
empower them to create lasting change in their own lives and communities. Seeking to put God's love into action,
Habitat for Humanity brings people together to build homes, communities and hope.

OUR ACTIVITY

Based on donation from individuals, organization, companies, churches and
many others around the world, we render the following service to the
struggling widows in this part of the world;

Advocacy

Your Involvement Makes an Impact. Be a voice to the voiceless, by talking to places that may be able to help spread the word. One for all, all for one. Do something kind to a struggling widow around you. Together we can make the world a better place.

Partnership

Partnering with the Rural farmers. Most struggling widows are farmers, and they face difficulties like; insufficient income, unawareness to modern technology and market demand. We collaborate with partners to improve these measures.

Health Care

Caring for the sick. We ensure senior struggling widows with health situations receive appropriate medication. We provide medication box with compartments marked by the days and time to take doses. This can help simplify their medication taking process.

Education

Educating and Giving hope to these struggling widows. We build their hopes, making them to believe in their dreams, even though it's tough but they are tougher. Uplifting them up in prayer and the hope to have a better new day.

Spiritual Upliftment

Building their spiritual lives. We make them to have a spiritual attachment to God. We help them understand the principal objective of faith, the conceivement of God, as well as His eternal and inevitable existence.

Counseling

With the COVID-19 outbreak, we extend counseling in the following: COVID-19 vaccination, eat lots of fruits and vegetables, limit excessive consumption of sugar, increase in physical activities, limit alcohol intake.

Donations

We provide financial assistance, provision of necessary resources like; agricultural equipments, business support, career orietation books and many more, in order to help them improve on their wellbeing.

Home Assistance

We help with daily activities such as; sanitation and environmental cleaning, meal preparation with essential diet consideration, medication and monitoring of vitals such as; blood pressure, glucose and a few more.

Fund Raising

We raise funding for community improvement and quick response to environmental crisis and outbreaks. We help improve the community to help these struggling widows fa-envelopesafe, and have a sense of belonging to our society,

Words from a Co-Founder

Everything big starts little.

My vision is to provide specialized holistic services for our struggling widows. To be unique in approach to care and support our old struggling widows, by utilizing a specialized holistic approach. There is also a great focus on emotional and educational support to assist them in reaching their goals and exhausting their potential in them. I am fundamentally committed to our senior struggling widows, with the intention that let no struggling widow go to bed hungry or suffer because of lack of medical attention, irrespective of her race, religion or tribe. Together we can make the world a better place.



Princewell Ambe Ambe

Co-Founder & President
