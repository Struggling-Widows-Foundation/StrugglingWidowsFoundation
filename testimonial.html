OUR TESTIMONIAL

Rahel, Struggling Widow.

I am Rahel, I lost my husband 25years ago. Being just a housewife, things were not the best on my part especially in bringing up the children. Its was more of nightmare situation. We were barely surviving since the load on me was so heavy, no one to really support me especially with the children. Farming was the main activity to support us and as God will always have it. I was able to sponsor some of the children through out their elementary school. It was a journey of good , bad and ugly moments. Thank God for Joan (of blessed memory) who introduced me to SWF (my second husband). I pray God will keep replenishing the source of this foundation. And for you Joan, may your soul continue to rest in peace till we meet one day to part no more.

Helen, Struggling Widow.

My name is Helen. I was 4months pregnant 9years ago when I lost the source of my bone. Life for me wasn't worth living. I knew I was going to die. it was for my sake that he went fetching water that night and was bitten by a snake. Things became tough for my unborn child and I. Just few days after the burial, my husband family didn't allow me to mourn him for a week, they accused me of killing their brother. As a result, I was kicked out of the house, all the pigs, goats my husband kept were confiscated by them. Me and my unborn were left with nothing, I went back to my brother’s house who was a catechist . It was really rough on me, I kept imagining how servants were riding on houses and kings were trekking, my husband family was reaping what my husband sowed while I was barely surviving in my brother’s house, what kept me going was the word of wisdom my brother told me” Helen! When your cloud is full, your rain will fall” behold my rain fell one faithful Thursday. On my way from the farm, I met a group of ladies discussing about SWF. It caught my attention, I joined them, weeks later, I joined SWF, and since then this foundation has been of great help to me morally, physically, spiritually and financially. My prayer is; this foundation should move from strength to strength as it helps with the plight of these vulnerable widows like me.

Florence, Struggling Widow.

My name is Florence, I am 28years, I lost my husband last year. I just want to thank SWF for my sewing machine, it is really helping me and the children.

Struggling Widows Foundation - SWF

The SWF is a non profit organization, non denomination organization which strive to enable that widows have easy access to health-care expertise that are beyond their reach, empowering them, advice, support and bring them closer to their heavenly creator (GOD).
